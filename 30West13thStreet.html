Loft living in the heart of Greenwich Village.

Set within a historic Greenwich Village loft building, Residence 4A at 30 West 13th Street offers approximately 1,200 square feet of authentic architectural character and the quiet sophistication today’s most discerning buyers are seeking. In a market moving away from sterile white-box condominiums, this expansive downtown home offers warmth, texture, and emotional presence. This is not performative luxury — this is lived-in luxury.

The Residence

The oversized one-bedroom suite offers the proportions of a classic Junior 4, with generous living and dining areas that easily accommodate a home office, library, or guest space. Soaring 10-foot ceilings, two full bathrooms, and oversized north-facing windows fill the home with abundant natural light throughout the day. The spacious bedroom features floor-to-ceiling closets, while custom built-ins and rich wood millwork add both elegance and functionality. Central heating and air conditioning, recessed lighting with dimmers, a custom built-in washer/dryer closet, and a digital security system with mobile connectivity complete a home designed for the way people live today.

Why This Home Resonates

Authentic loft character. Warm wood millwork, built-in shelving, generous proportions, and a sense of architectural permanence create a home that feels distinctly New York.

A true downtown sanctuary. Despite its central location near Union Square, Flatiron, and the West Village, the residence offers an atmosphere of calm, privacy, and separation from the intensity of Midtown life.

Designed for modern professional living. Ideal for hybrid work, intimate entertaining, and quiet evenings, the layout naturally supports both productivity and decompression — a fit for executives, attorneys, creatives, and longtime New Yorkers who value refinement without excess.

The Building

Located within a historic Greenwich Village loft building, Residence 4A offers privacy and quiet enjoyment via a secure digital intercom with controlled building access, allowing residents to grant entry remotely. Amenities include an elevator and wheelchair accessibility, and the brick façade has been professionally repointed and resealed, preserving its historic character. Private storage and bike storage are available for lease through the building, subject to availability and waitlists.

The Neighborhood & Transit

Just steps from Union Square and the 14th Street transit hub, with unbeatable access to shopping, dining, and downtown energy — moments from Washington Square Park, the Union Square Greenmarket, Flatiron, and the West Village, and surrounded by top institutions including NYU, The New School, and Parsons. The 14th Street hub puts the 4, 5, 6, L, N, Q, R, W, F, M, 1, 2, and 3 lines at your door.

Lovingly maintained by its current owner for approximately 50 years, Residence 4A reflects a pride of ownership that is increasingly rare — a rare opportunity to own an authentic Greenwich Village loft defined by generous proportions, timeless design, and enduring appeal.
